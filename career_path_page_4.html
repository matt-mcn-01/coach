Career Path In Development
What this page will become
Career Path will sit alongside the FCDO People Capability matrix to help you reflect on where you're growing, what's stagnating, and which conversations are worth having with your manager. Building this well needs data we don't yet have access to — performance reviews, learning records, and your own self-assessment over time.
Planned Capabilities
Capability Matrix Tracking Planned
Self-assessed progress against each capability area, updated quarterly, visualised as a radar chart so you can see where you're broadening versus deepening.
Learning Time Audit MVP candidate
How much of your week was actually spent on learning or development activity — derived from calendar tags and protected blocks, not assumed.
Growth Conversation Prompts Planned
Discussion prompts for your next 1:1 with your manager, generated from your own reflections — never from surveillance signals about your work.
Quarterly Reflection Planned
A short journal-style review you write yourself; the Coach responds with questions to help you draw the right lessons, not unsolicited judgement.
